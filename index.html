meet Guardiowl,

Mr.GuardiOwl will save your children. Your children's new Chief of Security. Don't worry.

  • Help Them Live endeavors to deliver food & other basic necessities to the most vulnerable sectors of the North Korean society, especially orphaned children, school children in impoverished areas, the handicapped, the elderly and single parents. Every effort is made to find partners of the highest integrity with the utmost in transparent monitoring methods available under admittedly difficult circumstances. Most food aid, at present, is in the form of baked bread rolls.
  • Help Them Live also endeavors to provide shelter, food, very basic health and hygiene assistance to North Korean refugees in China. It does so based on need only and without prejudice of the refugee’s creed or lack thereof. However, it is our belief and operating principle that spiritual hope, faith and direction are of equal importance to physical sustenance. Each refugee in our shelters receives a Bible and an introduction to the Christian faith. Acceptance is a personal matter.
